Behaviour Driven Development (BDD) is about to simplfy the communication between different shareholders such as business analysts, developers, testers and customers. Thus while the test cases are written, the tester uses a more simplified language according to test driven development approach. So that all the shareholders can read  and understand the given scenarios. To provide this simplicity, tester includes 3 key elements in his/her scenarios. While the word GIVEN defines the context, WHEN describes the action taken by a spesific user. The word THEN is the expected result of the action.
